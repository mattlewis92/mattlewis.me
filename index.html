About me

I am a hard working and self motivated software developer with 7 years experience, 3 of which are commercial. My main area of expertise is the evolving web and everything related to it.

My key skills are in JavaScript (AngularJS and Node.JS), HTML5, CSS3, PHP, MongoDB and MySQL. I also have a lot of experience in deploying and scaling linux servers for high traffic volume websites. Recently I have started developing hybrid apps for iOS and Android using the Ionic framework.
